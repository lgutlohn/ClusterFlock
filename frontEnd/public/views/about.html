Cluster Flock

Data cluster system

Eric Smith, Kathleen Lew, Lauren Gutlohn, Jesse Everett

Trending Analytics

First Edition

Introduction

This application allows a user (or users) to cluster specific nouns used in tweets in regard to certain events.

Importance

In order to keep up to date with current events, it's important to analyze naming conventions and associations with trending news. Cluster Flock features a visual representation of commonly used words and gives users a true measure of statistical data.

Example

Say during the state of the union address by Barak Obama people were to live tweet the event
  • President Obama may be phrased different ways
  • President Obama
  • The 44th President of the United States of America
  • Barry
And the user wanted to cluster all of these for analysis A username would be entered and the tweets would appear and could then be clustered together There could be more than one cluster at a time. Say one wanted to complie data from the entire address There could be a cluster for Vice President Biden
  • a cluster for Speaker Boehner
  • a cluster for random congressman interjecting the speech
